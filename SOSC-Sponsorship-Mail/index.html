What is DevHost?

It is our annual student developer meetup in Mangalore where students, developers, young tech enthusiasts come together to take part in hands-on workshops, overnight hackathons over several cups of coffee.

Here are some of the many things to expect from DevHost!

01 Overnight Hackathon

Problem statements cover a wide set of issues allowing the participants to get the best experience in coming up with a solution using any sort of technologies they know. A stipulated amount of time is given to plan, design and implement said solution to the best of their abilities.

02 Networking

Through this event we aim to connect students with the industry by hosting developers and industrial experts from across the nation. During hackathons, we follow a mentor-mentee system where each team is mentored by a subject expert.

03 Hands-on Workshops

Our workshops focus on introducing new technologies to students to keep them updated to what industries are currently working with which helps the students understand the topics in-depth.
